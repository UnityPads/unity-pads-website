About Unity Pads

Delivering homes with purpose and long-term value.

Unity Pads Ltd is a UK property development and housing supply company. We identify opportunities to acquire, improve and develop residential property, including existing homes, land and new-build schemes.

Our aim is to deliver safe, attractive and well-considered homes while becoming a dependable long-term partner to housing associations, local authorities and other housing providers.

We combine a commercial understanding of property with a clear focus on quality, communication and the needs of the communities our homes serve.
